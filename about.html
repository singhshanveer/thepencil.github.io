About The Pencil

Established in 2023, we are the largest pencil manufacturer in India. Besides being a households name in Canada, our brand The Pencil is exported to over 50 countries.

Our statr-of-art manufacturing units with latest machinery set up daily produce 8 million pencils, 1.5 million sharpners, 2.5 million erasers.

Manufacturing

Our goods are known for their dependability, therefore we manufacture them with the strictest quality controls supported by years of research and development.

Our 10 factories, which are dispersed over 5 different cities in India, use the greatest raw materials and the most recent technology to produce goods of unrivalled quality. To guarantee uncompromising quality standards, every tiny component—including screws, sharpener blades, and pencil lacquer—is produced on site.

sustainable business

We do not use any forest wood for manufacturing pencils. Our concern for the environment has led us to develop our own plantations.

Moreover, we procure wood from the farmers who either grow trees on their farm, land or in the courtyard of their residential premises.

This practice ensures an uninterrupted supply of wood of consistent quality. It also helps protect bio-diversity and maintain ecological balance. Constant efforts are made by the company to improve processes and maximize the utilization of inputs to prevent the degradation of the environment.
All ingredients used by the company are non-toxic in nature. Research and Development efforts are also being made to switch over to eco-friendly water-based lacquers and PVC-free formulations.
